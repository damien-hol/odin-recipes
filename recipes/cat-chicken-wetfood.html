100% meat!!!

Ingrediants

  • Chicken
  • Water
  • and that is all!!!

I use an instant pot but you can of course cook it in a regular pot, cooking time may vary

how to?

  1. Put the chicken in the pot with a little bit of water
  2. Set the instant pot to poultry option for 15 min
  3. Let perssure relese naturally
  4. Open lid and let it cool down for a few minutes
  5. Transfer everything in a mixer or blender
  6. Mix until the meat is all shreded
  7. For longer conservation, scoop the preparation in an ice cube tray and let it freeze
  8. Ones frozen tranfer the cubes into an bag

When it comes to giving her the food, I put a cube in a plate and leave it 45 seconds in the microwave to thaw.

After those 45 seconds, I add some water to the preparation and mixe it all to make sure that everyting is at room temperature.
