Chocolate Brownies

Chocolat brownies along with Crepes are my go to deserts to make, classic yet hard to mess up

There is really not much to say about brownies exept that most people love them!!

So I'll just dive right in:

Ingrediants:

  • 200gr of chocolete
  • 150gr of salted butter
  • 150gr of brown sugar
  • 60 gr od flour
  • 3 eggs
  • Vanilla extract

For this recipe, you will also need

  • A pot to melt the chocolate and butter
  • A small bowl
  • A bigger mixing bowl
  • A medium size cooking pan
  • A mesuring cup
  • A scale
  • A wisk
  • A small or butter knife

How to:

  1. Preheat the oven at 180 degre celcius
  2. Cut the butter and the chocolate into cubes and melt it in a pot on low fire or bain marie
  3. Spread a small cube in cooking pan and set it asside for later
  4. In a small bowl, whisk the eggs
  5. Once the butter and chocolate is melted and mixed together, pour it in a mixing bowl
  6. Mix in the the sugar, flour, vanilla extract and eggs (make sure that the preparation is not too hot before adding the egg)
  7. Once everything is mixed together, pour the preparation in the cooking pan
  8. Let it cook for about 15 minutes
  9. Once out of the oven, let it rest for at least 15 minutes before enjoying
